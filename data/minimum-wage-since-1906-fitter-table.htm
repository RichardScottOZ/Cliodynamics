[2] Converted in real terms using the RBA Inflation Calculator (www.rba.gov.au/calculator/) as at April 2021.

[3] Source: Federal Basic Wage, 1922-1950 (shillings per week), Professor Keith Hancock, The Hon. Reg Hamilton. [4] The fitter wage 1967 onwards is taken from the orders hyperlinked to The History of the Australian Minimum Wage, The Hon. Reg Hamilton. The 1966 amount is taken from Six Wage Concepts, J.Hutson, p.97.

[5] From 1968-1997 the Victorian Metal Industry minimum wage is used, which was influential but there were a range of rates, rates sometimes being lower. From 1998 onwards the new Federal Minimum Wage is used and has application to all awards.
